Psilocybe cyanescens

Wavy Caps are renowned for high psilocybin content and intense bluing reactions.

Principal Constituents

Compound Typical Range Notes
Psilocybin 0.9 – 1.8% dry weight Among the most concentrated psilocybin levels recorded in wild Psilocybe species.
Psilocin 0.1 – 0.3% dry weight Blue bruising is rapid due to high psilocin and baeocystin conversion.
Baeocystin & Norbaeocystin Trace – 0.2% dry weight combined Contribute to overall psychoactivity; individual effects remain under study.

Pharmacology & Metabolism

  • Rapid conversion of psilocybin to psilocin leads to swift onset when consumed.
  • Effects often peak within 60–90 minutes and can last 4–6 hours depending on dose and metabolism.
  • Potency can vary widely between flushes; homogenize dried material before measuring portions.

Toxicology

  • Acute Effects: Intense visual alterations and potential for overwhelming experiences at moderate doses.
  • Physiological Response: Mild tachycardia, pupil dilation, occasional gastrointestinal discomfort.
  • Environmental Toxins: Urban wood chips may accumulate contaminants; avoid specimens from treated lumber or polluted sites.
