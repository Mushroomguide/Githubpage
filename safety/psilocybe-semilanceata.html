Psilocybe semilanceata

Guidance for legal awareness, handling, and responsible storage of Liberty Caps.

Legal Considerations

  • Psilocybin-containing mushrooms are scheduled or controlled in many countries. Verify national and regional laws before collecting.
  • Private land requires owner permission. Public lands often prohibit removal of fungi.
  • Transporting fresh specimens across borders can trigger criminal penalties.

Collection Practices

  • Use a clean, breathable container such as a paper bag to prevent condensation.
  • Document habitat and take photographs for later verification by experienced mycologists.
  • Avoid harvesting in areas treated with pesticides or livestock wormers.

Drying & Storage

  • Spread thin layers over food-safe dehydrator trays set below 40 °C to preserve psilocybin.
  • Store thoroughly dried mushrooms in airtight containers with desiccant packs, away from light and heat.
  • Label containers clearly and keep out of reach of children and pets.

Harm Reduction

  • Never consume unidentified mushrooms. Seek second opinions from credible field guides or community experts.
  • Individuals with mental health conditions or cardiovascular issues should abstain without medical supervision.
  • Have a trusted, sober sitter present if ingestion occurs; start with very low amounts due to high potency.
